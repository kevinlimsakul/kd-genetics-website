## "Seed to Spliff" — 90-second storyboard

### What it is

A 90-second silent cinematic video that explains the KD premium without a single word. Visitor walks away understanding: *this is grown by hand, on a tiny island, in organic living soil, by a family that cares.* No voiceover. No on-screen text until the final logo. Music + ambient only.

A **30-second cutdown** for Instagram / TikTok reels is edited from the same footage, not re-shot.

### Shot-by-shot breakdown

| Time | Shot | Beat | Direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0:00 – 0:08 | Drone flying in over Tanote Bay, passing mountain, skimming jungle canopy, slowing to reveal the farm from above | *This is where it starts. The island. The isolation. The small-batch reality.* | 4K 60fps. Morning haze preferred. One continuous clip over cuts. |
| 0:08 – 0:12 | Hard cut: macro of **Papa's hand holding a single seed** | *Human scale. Heritage.* | Shallow depth of field. Golden-hour side light. Show rings, skin, age. |
| 0:12 – 0:16 | Slow-motion: seed drops from his fingers | *Cinematic punctuation* | 120fps retimed. Black velvet or dark cloth underneath. Drop height 20cm max. |
| 0:16 – 0:22 | Macro: seed landing on **wet tissue on a plain dinner plate** | *The homemade start. Every bud begins on a plate in a kitchen.* | Plain matte white or earthenware plate, no pattern. Water droplets on tissue catch light. Shoot top-down + 45° — pick in edit. |
| 0:22 – 0:25 | Cut to black — 2 seconds darkness, ambient sound only | *Compress time. Build tension.* | Subtle sound design OK in post: one water droplet, one crow. |
| 0:25 – 0:32 | Papa's hands enter frame and unveil the plate — now a **sprouted seed with taproot** | *The miracle. Quiet reveal.* | Daniel prepares a sprouted seed on a plate the night before, kept in darkness under cloth. Papa unveils on camera. One directional warm light, rest of frame dark. |
| 0:32 – 0:40 | Close on Papa's face as he looks down at the sprout | *The emotional anchor of the whole video* | Eyes only, no wide shot. Natural reaction. Shoot 5 takes, pick the half-smile. |
| 0:40 – 0:48 | Three fast cuts: sprout → soil block → small pot → larger pot | *Rhythmic growth. Pace up.* | Similar background across all three for visual rhyme. Bio Bizz bottle visible in one (sponsorship + organic proof). |
| 0:48 – 0:55 | Montage of growth stages: seedling → veg → early flower → late flower, each clip 1.5–2 sec | *Time passing. The work.* | Daniel walks Hue plant to plant — each stage exists on the farm already. Morning soft light. Match exposure in edit. |
| 0:55 – 1:03 | Macro of trichomes on a mature bud, slow focus pull | *Product moment. Why it's worth what it is.* | 10× macro. Resin visible. Natural light preferred; reflector if needed. |
| 1:03 – 1:10 | Hands trimming a bud (Daniel or team), then bud going into a cure jar | *Small batch, human touch* | Overhead on the wooden slab in the shop. Uses existing shop interior. |
| 1:10 – 1:18 | Cure jar opening in Papa's hands, bud lifted onto rolling tray, joint being rolled | *The craft.* | Shop counter under warm pendant bulb, or outdoor wooden table. Close on hands only. |
| 1:18 – 1:25 | Papa brings joint to mouth, lighter click, flame, first draw | *The payoff begins.* | Gimbal pull-in from medium to close. Flame catches light. |
| 1:25 – 1:30 | Wide shot: Papa exhales, slow-motion smoke, golden backlight, Tanote Bay blurred in background | *Landing frame. This is the brand.* | **This is the hero frame for the entire site.** 120fps retimed. Minimum 5 takes. |

### Production notes for Hue

- **No voiceover.** Music + ambient only. Music sourced after edit.
- **No on-screen text** until the final logo.
- **Color grade:** warm earthy — cream, golden, olive. Lift shadows slightly. Avoid teal-orange Hollywood look.
- **Shoot for editability:** multiple takes on each beat; varied focal lengths for edit options.
- **Capture ambient sound on set** even if unused (birds, wind, leaves).
